## Project principles

- Maintain an independent repository and lifecycle.
- Keep coupling to other Dokoni projects explicit and as limited as practical.
- Define architecture only after a traceable project definition.
- Maintain confirmed public information in the project repository.

AMTS may structure project knowledge but defines neither Akari CE nor its software architecture.
